Partner & Trial Lawyer

Ryan Loya

If you've been injured and are looking for an attorney who knows what to do next, stop searching. In just 10 years, Ryan Loya has secured nearly $200 million in jury verdicts and millions more in settlements for clients, making him one of Houston's most effective and respected trial lawyers. Hundreds of clients have placed their futures in Ryan's hands and he has delivered.

A new-generation trial lawyer, Ryan understands how to connect with clients and juries from all backgrounds. He uses cutting-edge courtroom technology, compelling visuals, and clear storytelling to make even the most complex cases simple.

Ryan has earned a reputation for restoring peace of mind, getting clients back on their feet, and getting them the best outcome. No billboards. No gimmicks. No TikTok dances. Just work.

The Loya name carries weight in the insurance industry and for good reason. Ryan's family background gives him rare insight into how insurers evaluate risk, how adjusters think, and how defense lawyers strategize. Whether he's negotiating for clients or arguing their case to a jury, you'll have a lawyer who tells your story to get you the result you want.

From his days as a two-sport athlete to becoming a decorated advocacy competitor at South Texas College of Law, Ryan knows the work it takes to win. After earning his bachelor's degree from Louisiana State University, he went on to excel as a varsity mock trial, moot court, and arbitration advocate, winning multiple awards in the nation's premier advocacy program. And just two months after earning his law license, he was already in the courtroom winning cases.

Today, Mr. Loya focuses on commercial auto wrecks, wrongful death, workplace injuries, offshore incidents, gas explosions, and legal malpractice cases, recovering millions for injured individuals and families who refuse to settle for less.

If you want a lawyer who will fight for you like family, strategize like an insider, and deliver results, your search is over.
