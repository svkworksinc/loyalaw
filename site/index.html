About the Firm

The Loya Law Firm

The Loya Law Firm, based in Houston, Texas, focuses on significant personal injury cases involving wrongful death, traumatic brain injuries, truck and 18-wheeler wrecks, refinery and plant explosions, workplace injuries, and other acts of negligence. We approach every case with precision, persistence, and the determination to win for our clients.

When someone's life has been turned upside down by injury or loss, they deserve a firm that will fight with skill, conviction, and the courage to never settle for less. We maximize results, and just as importantly, we treat every client with the attention, care, integrity, and respect they deserve.
